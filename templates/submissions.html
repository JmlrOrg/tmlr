TMLR Submission Instructions

We are not yet accepting submissions to TMLR!

We expect to be up and running by end of March; stay tuned for updates. The guidance below will apply once we start receiving submissions.

We will be using the reviewing and publication systems on OpenReview for openness and transparency.

Before submitting to TMLR, please carefully read the Submission guidelines and editorial policies page page which explains TMLRâ€™s scope, criteria, and review process, the Author Guidelines, and also the Code of Conduct and Ethical Guidelines. The Author Guidelines page contains a LaTeX stylefile and template; these are mandatory. Any changes to the stylefile or template that alters the formatting, font, or layout of the manuscript may result in rejection without review.

Once your manuscript is correctly formatted and you have read the TMLR requirements, especially regarding the policy on dual submissions and self-plagiarism, and the ethical guidelines which may require a broader impact statement, proceed to the OpenReview TMLR site to begin the submission process.

You will receive an email once your submission has been assigned to an action editor; this will mark the beginning of the review process.
